12
Fields of wheat
Who’s that running through the fields of wheat?
There goes the vicar’s daughter with sandals on her feet
The Farmer saw her and his face turned red
“I’m not too pleased about that” he said

Who’s that whistling as he walks out the door?
There goes Silly Billy from the grocer’s store
He’s off to dinner with the fat prize pig
dressed up in a frock and an auburn wig

Who’s that marching to the London Exchange?
It’s Little Lord Investor with his pockets full of change
He’s heading to his broker to buy a dozen shares
And if he doesn’t see a good return he’ll box him round the ears

Who’s that causing a kerfuffle over there?
It’s Good King Feffel with his ruffled yellow hair
He gets caught up in every sort of trifling affair
But he won’t own up to anything: “That’s piffle, I declare!”
